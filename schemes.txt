NREGA Scheme (MGNREGA): Guarantees 100 days of wage employment per financial year to any rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. To apply, contact your local Gram Panchayat. Wages are directly credited to the bank account linked with the job card. Delays in payment should be reported to the Panchayat.

National Pension Scheme (NPS): A government-sponsored pension scheme. Subscribers contribute regularly during their working life. On retirement, they can withdraw a part of the corpus and use the rest to receive a regular pension.
